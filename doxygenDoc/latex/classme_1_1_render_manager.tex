\doxysubsection{Detailed Description}
Ogre\+Manager initialize \mbox{\hyperlink{namespace_ogre}{Ogre}} (Root, RTShader\+System, \mbox{\hyperlink{classme_1_1_scene_manager}{Scene\+Manager}}, \mbox{\hyperlink{classme_1_1_render_window}{Render\+Window}}), locate and load resources (from resource.\+cfg) and handle the creation of camera, light, mesh You can access the Ogre\+Manager just calling om().
