\doxysubsection{Detailed Description}
\mbox{\hyperlink{classme_1_1_sound_manager}{Sound\+Manager}} provides \mbox{\hyperlink{namespace_f_m_o_d}{FMOD}} wrappers to manage audio creation and modulation, audio channel and audio channel groups. You can access the \mbox{\hyperlink{classme_1_1_input_manager}{Input\+Manager}} calling sm().
